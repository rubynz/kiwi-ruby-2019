Kiwi Ruby

We want to support people with different voices and ideas to attend Kiwi Ruby

Apply for a scholarship here

The Ruby community should reflect the diversity and vibrancy of New Zealand, and people from underrepresented backgrounds should have the opportunity to participate fully in our community.

Thanks to Ackama, we are able to offer Diversity Scholarships for people who would otherwise not be able to afford to make it to Kiwi Ruby, but would benefit from attending.

Scholarships cover the cost of a ticket and we may be able to cover your travel costs within New Zealand. Anyone is welcome to apply, but we’ll prioritise people from groups underrepresented in the Ruby community. Applications close Monday 30th of September, and we’ll contact all applicants by Monday 7th of October.
